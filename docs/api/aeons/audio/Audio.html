interface Audio

package aeons.audio

Audio is used to play sounds and vibrate a device.

Variables

masterVolume:Float

The master volume multiplier to adjust the volume globally. Value between 0.0 and 1.0.

Methods

@:value({ loop : false, volume : 1.0 })addChannel(sound:Sound, volume:Float = 1.0, loop:Bool = false):SoundChannel

Add a sound channel to the audio manager.

Parameters:

sound

The sound asset for the channel.

volume

The sound volume.

loop

Does the sound loop.

Returns:

The created sound channel.

mute():Void

Mute all audio.

pauseAll():Void

Pause all audio.

removeChannel(channel:SoundChannel):Void

Remove a channel from the audio manager.

Parameters:

sound

The channel to remove.

resumeAll():Void

Resume all audio after pausing it.

stopAll():Void

Stop all audio. This sets the start of all sound channels back to the start.

unMute():Void

UnMute all audio.

vibrate(time:Int):Void

Vibrate the device.

Parameters:

time

The time to vibrate in milliseconds. (Does nothing on iOS)
